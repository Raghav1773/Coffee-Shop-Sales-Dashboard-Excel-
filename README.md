# ☕ Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard

## About
End-to-end Coffee Shop Sales Analysis project built entirely in Excel, focusing on transaction-level data to uncover sales patterns, customer behavior, and product performance through interactive dashboards and pivot-based analysis.

---

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes coffee shop sales data to identify key business trends such as peak sales hours, product performance, store-wise revenue, and customer ordering patterns. The entire analysis is performed using Excel, leveraging pivot tables, data cleaning, and dashboard visualization techniques to generate actionable insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze hourly sales patterns and peak business hours  
- Identify top-performing products and categories  
- Understand sales distribution across store locations  
- Study customer ordering behavior by day and size  
- Visualize insights through an interactive Excel dashboard  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Microsoft Excel**
  - Pivot Tables  
  - Charts (Bar, Line, Pie)  
  - Data Cleaning  
  - Dashboard Design  
- **Dataset (CSV/Excel format)**

## 📈 Key Analysis Highlights
- Identified peak sales hours between morning and mid-day periods  
- Coffee category contributes the highest percentage of total sales  
- Large and Regular sizes dominate order distribution  
- Barista Espresso and Brewed Chai Tea are top-selling products  
- Store-wise analysis shows consistent performance across locations

## 💡 Business Insights
- Helps optimize staffing during peak hours  
- Supports inventory planning based on product demand  
- Enables better product positioning and promotions  
- Assists in understanding customer preferences and buying behavior
